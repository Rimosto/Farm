About Rimomo Farm

Founded in 2015, Rimomo Farm is a privately-owned avocado farm located in Uplands, Kenya. We specialize in growing Hass and Fuerte avocados, nurturing seedlings for farmers, and producing high-quality avocado-based products. Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture while providing customers with fresh, healthy produce straight from the source.
